Research

I'm really interested in the use of software to provide rich and interactive learning experiences. As a tutor I believe there is no substitute for real human interaction, but I am currently studying the principles of adaptive learning, where students consolidate what they have learned in a lesson with a series of problems to solve that adapt based on the users' performance. This combined with gamification, is a means by which to focus students' attention on problems they need to work on, as opposed to a typical situation where a student may only practise solving problems with which they feel comfortable.
